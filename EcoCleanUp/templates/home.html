EcoCleanUp supports community-driven sustainability by making cleanup activities easy to organise and easier to participate in. The platform helps bring local volunteers together to remove rubbish from public spaces and promotes responsible waste handling through consistent event records and reporting.

By capturing participation, outcomes, and feedback, EcoCleanUp helps communities understand impact over time and encourages ongoing environmental stewardship — one cleanup at a time.
